Musica Donavania, 25 February 2017 - Jon Stringer Tribute

This event was a tribute to Jon Stringer and his fabulous music collection. It featured Medieval music of Spain and the Sephardic Diaspora, all from that collection. We have posted a Playlist of the music presented. The dishes served were all Spanish and Sephardic.
