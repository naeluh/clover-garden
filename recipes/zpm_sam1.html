Musica Donavania, 27 April 2013 - Refugees

All the music for this event was composed by 20th century refugees from Europe and the United States, and some by folks who didn't make it out in time. All the dishes served at this event were from South America, where many refugees found a new home. We have posted a Playlist of the music presented.
