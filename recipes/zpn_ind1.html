Worldwide Party Menus

These are party menus I've executed and served at Musica Donavania events hosted in my home. I have done these entirely myself, which does take careful planning and organization - they would be more easily done as a team effort. For events hosted by others, I usually provide one main dish, and possibly a side dish. Many more regional recipes will be found in the indexes linked to in the column to the left.
