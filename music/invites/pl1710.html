Musica Donavania Playlist


October 28, 2017 - Amazon

In October we feature music from the countries of the Amazon: Peru, Bolivia and Brazil - west to the Pacific and as far south as Rio.This is a very diverse region, including substantial native populations (some not yet contacted) and large Old World populations: Spanish, Portuguese, Italians, French, Germans, Africans, and more recently, Japanese.
