Musica Donavania - 21 Nov 2009

This was our annual St. Cecilia's day tribute to the music and life of Henry Purcell, held at the Altadena location. For this event, we had both recorded and live music. A whole roasted turkey and many other fine dishes were served, including a Sicilian Rabbit Stew (but made with more economical chicken thighs) which was greatly liked. The traditional midnight ceremony was held, as usual.
