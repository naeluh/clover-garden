Musica Donavania - April 19, 2014

Our traditional "anti-Bach" event featured music by two . . ummm . . "excentric" composers. Why just two when there are so many? We have time only for Gerald Tyrwhitt-Wilson, 14th Baron Berners and Percy Grainger in one evening, but we'll get to Eric Satie and a few of the others at upcoming events. We have posted a Playlist of the music presented.

Food Notes:   The dishes served were all of Philippine origin, and were much enjoyed. We have a Pictorial Menu of the dishes, linking with recipe pages for each one.
