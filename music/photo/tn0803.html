Musica Donavania - 29 March 2008

Held at the La Crescenta location this gathering departed somewhat from the Bach's Birghday Month tradition. While some pieces by J.S. Bach were featured, it was not an all Bach event. The remainder of the evening was thrown open for what people wanted to share. We started with Saint-Saens and ended with Steeleye Span, with Peter Maxwell Davies (Mavis in Las Vegas), Randall Thompson and Eric Satie in between The event was well attended and there was plenty of food, including a Chicken curry, Celery à la Grecque and Dong'an Chicken.
