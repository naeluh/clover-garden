Musica Donavania - 26 February 2011

Our winter theme continued with music of Scandinavia, a place where, unlike here, they actually have winter. This very successful gathering was held at the La Crescenta location.   Food Notes:   Scandinavian food was served. Many open faced sandwiches were made and consumed as well as the main prepared dishes. We have posted a Pictorial Menu with links to the recipes.
