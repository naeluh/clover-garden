Musica Donavania Photos   -   December 31, 2016

This was our annual Christmas is Over! / New Year's Eve event at the La Crescenta location. The music was mostly Igor Stravinsky, with one piece each for Carl Orff, Bela Bartok and Modest Mussorgsky - no Christmas music whaterver. Dishes served were all Chinese, since that's what the host was working with at the time. We have posted a Pictorial Menu with links to the recipes, and also a Playlist.
