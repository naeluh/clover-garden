Musica Donavania - November 22 2014

This was our annual Saint Cecelia's day event, celebrating the works of Henry Purcell, the last great English composer until almost the dawn of the 20th century, held at the Altadena location.   Food Notes:   We have posted a link to the recipe for Beef Stew with Chestnuts which was served at the event. This stew is Elizabethan, but was probably still served in Purcell's time.
