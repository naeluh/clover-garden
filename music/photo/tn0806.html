Musica Donavania - 28 June 2008

This event was held at the La Crescenta location, and featured works of current composers working in Australia and New Zealand. In keeping with Australian "fusion cuisine" an Indonesian Chicken Curry and Pinapple and Cucumber Salad were served, along with many other potables and comestibles.
