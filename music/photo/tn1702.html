Musica Donavania Photos - February 25, 2017

This event was a tribute to Jon Stringer, and his fabulous music collection. It featured Medieval music from Spain and the Sephardic Diaspora, all from his collection. The few photos we have were from early in the evening before all the guests had arrived. We have posted a Pictorial Menu with links to the recipes, and also a Playlist.
