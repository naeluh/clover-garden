Musica Donavania - December?, 2000

Held at the Northridge locatione, the home of Karen and David Miodowski, the exact date is questionable, but placed in December because Ron Cobb has not yet returned to Australia, and there is no evidence it was a St. Cecilia event (November). It is also a meeting where we have photos of Jon Stringer attending after his stroke (in wheelchair). Unfortunately, the photos were taken with outdoor film.
